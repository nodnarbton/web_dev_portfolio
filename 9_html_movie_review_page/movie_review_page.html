Movie Review: K-Pop Demon Hunters

K-Pop Demon Hunters is a 2025 animated musical film and pop culture sensation about a K-pop girl group who lead double lives as demon hunters, facing off against a rival boy band of actual demons. The film stars the fictional group Huntr/x (Rumi, Mira, Zoey) and their adversaries, the Saja Boys, and premiered on Netflix in June 2025, quickly becoming a massive hit.
